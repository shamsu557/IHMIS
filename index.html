Welcome to Imam Hafsin Model International School

Imam Hafsin Model International School provides quality education across three distinct sections: Western Education, Islamiyya, and Tahfizz. With dedicated directors leading each section, our mission is to nurture future leaders through a blend of modern and traditional learning.

Western Education Section

Empowering students with modern education and life skills under the guidance of Mr. Muhammad Auwal

Islamiyya Section

Building a strong foundation in Islamic studies under the guidance of Mal. Usman Said

Tahfizz Section

Focusing on the memorization of the Holy Quran with leadership from Mal. Salihu Hashim
