Our History

Imam Hafsin Model International School is a private, registered institution certified by the Ministry of Education, Kano State. Established in 2014, the school also holds accreditation from the Nigerian Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC) and is registered for WAEC and NECO. The student population has grown from 20 in 2014 to over 307 today. Our school comprises three departments:

  • Tahfeez Section
  • Islamiyya and Arabic Department
  • Western Education Department: Offering both Science and Arts education.
