Imam Hafsin Model International School

Our History

Imam Hafsin Model International School was established in 1995 with the aim of providing holistic education that combines modern academic excellence with Islamic teachings. Starting as a small institution with only 50 students, the school has grown into a well-respected educational center, offering three distinct sections: Western Education, Islamiyya, and Tahfizz.

Over the years, the school has maintained its commitment to nurturing responsible and knowledgeable individuals who excel in both academic and moral spheres. It has received numerous awards for its outstanding contributions to education and remains a beacon of quality learning in the region.
