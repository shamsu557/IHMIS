Our History

Imam Hafsin Model International School is a private, registered institution that has been a beacon of knowledge since its establishment in 2014. Certified by the Ministry of Education, Kano State, and accredited by respected bodies such as the Nigerian Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC), the school is also registered for WAEC and NECO examinations, ensuring the highest standards in education. What started as a humble institution with only 20 students has grown significantly, now serving over 307 students, a testament to the trust and confidence the community has placed in the school’s educational approach.

The school operates with a unique and holistic educational structure that integrates both spiritual and academic growth. The curriculum is divided into three major departments: the Tahfeez Section, the Islamiyya and Arabic Department, and the Western Education Department, each offering distinct yet complementary programs designed to nurture students spiritually, intellectually, and socially.

The Tahfeez Section offers students the opportunity to memorize the Holy Quran while gaining deep understanding and appreciation of its teachings. This section emphasizes not only the memorization of Quranic verses but also their interpretation, strengthening the students' spiritual foundation.

The Islamiyya and Arabic Department is dedicated to enriching students' understanding of Islamic studies, including Fiqh (Islamic jurisprudence), Tafseer (Quranic interpretation), and proficiency in the Arabic language. This department ensures that students develop a deep connection with their cultural and religious heritage, equipping them with the tools to read, write, and speak Arabic fluently.

The Western Education Department focuses on academic excellence, offering both Science and Arts subjects that foster critical thinking, creativity, and problem-solving skills. With subjects ranging from Physics, Chemistry, and Biology to Literature, History, and Geography, this department ensures that students are well-prepared for the challenges of the modern world.

In its continuous effort to support the community, the school is introducing an Adult Education Program that focuses on literacy and vocational training. This program will cover areas such as ICT, Tailoring, and other technical skills, helping adults gain qualifications and skills for better job prospects. Additionally, the school is enhancing its entrepreneurial focus by offering training in various trades, preparing both students and adults for self-employment and business opportunities.

The school day is carefully structured to maintain a balance between academics, spirituality, and extracurricular activities. The Western Education classes run in the morning, followed by Islamiyya in the afternoon, while the Tahfeez classes take place on weekends. This schedule provides students with a well-rounded education that nurtures both their academic and spiritual growth.

Imam Hafsin Model International School operates from 7:30 AM to 5:30 PM, allowing students to engage deeply in their studies and extracurricular activities. This structure accommodates the diverse needs of students in both the Western Education and Islamiyya programs, with flexibility for the weekend Tahfeez section.

As we look to the future, Imam Hafsin Model International School remains committed to continuing its tradition of excellence. With plans to expand further, including the addition of more facilities and programs, the school is determined to offer its students an education that goes beyond textbooks, preparing them to lead successful and meaningful lives in their communities and the world.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide an education that balances academic excellence with Islamic values. We strive to develop responsible and knowledgeable individuals who are equipped with the skills and moral compass necessary to succeed in life and contribute positively to society.

Our Vision

We envision a future where students from Imam Hafsin Model International School emerge as confident, educated, and God-fearing individuals, capable of leading and serving their communities with wisdom, integrity, and compassion.
